# Research log

This append-only ledger records external architecture and compatibility
research without importing unreviewed code or license obligations into the
Apache-2.0 project.

## Research boundary

- Record the exact URL, access date, license, material read, and conclusion.
- Distinguish documentation/architecture research from source-code review.
- For AGPL or unclear/restrictive licenses, do not copy source, tests, protocol
  constants, fixtures, or identifiable implementation details. Any proposal to
  inspect or reuse such material requires a separate legal/license decision.
- A research conclusion is not Provider compatibility evidence. Reproducible
  Provider claims belong to the owning Spike and
  [compatibility matrix](PROVIDER_COMPATIBILITY.md).
- Update [third-party notices](../THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md) before any material
  reuse; an empty notices ledger is not permission to copy.
